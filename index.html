Your Projects

Coil Sim

A physics-driven playground that visualizes arcs leaping from a Tesla coil. Adjustable voltage sliders, realistic sound effects, and just enough danger to make you wonder if your monitor should've come with a Faraday cage.

Lightning.js

A JavaScript library that strikes your DOM with lightning bolts on demand. One function call, endless static energy. Subtle enough for UI effects, powerful enough to make your users feel slightly unsafe.

AC/DC Switcher

A cross-platform tool to flip seamlessly between alternating and direct flows. Ideal for devs who can't decide which current they're on. No guitar riffs built in-though the naming collision was entirely intentional.

Voltage Vibes

An experimental sound generator that translates voltage values into rhythmic pulses. Your console logs become dance tracks, proving that electricity has always had a beat-humans just tuned in late.

ResonanceOS

A conceptual operating system where every process vibrates at its own frequency and harmonizes with others. Less about multitasking, more about orchestration-think symphony, not scheduler.

Wardenclyffe Net

A peer-to-peer framework envisioned to cover the planet without cables. Elegant, ambitious, and tragically underfunded-just like the tower. Still, it connects your apps as if the dream had worked.

AmpereJS

A lightweight reactive framework where current flow drives state updates. Minimal syntax, maximal energy. Developers say it's fast; physicists say it violates conservation laws. Both are probably right.

Neon City

A glowing front-end theme pack inspired by turn-of-the-century electric fairs. Perfect for dashboards that want to look futuristic and retro simultaneously, without needing actual gas-discharge tubes.
